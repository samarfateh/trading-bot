# How to Access on Your Phone (Deployment Guide)

To access your **Calm Stock Tracker** on your phone or other computers, you need to put it on the web.
Since this is a simple "Static App", you can do this for **Free** in about 30 seconds.

## The Easiest Way: Netlify Drop

1.  **Open Netlify Drop**: Go to [https://app.netlify.com/drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop) in your browser.
2.  **Locate Your Folder**: Find the folder named **`$tock$`** on your Desktop.
3.  **Drag & Drop**: Drag the entire `$tock$` folder onto the "Drag and drop your site" box on the webpage.
4.  **Wait**: It will upload instantly.
5.  **You're Live!**: You will get a link like `https://funny-name-12345.netlify.app`.

## Install on Phone (App Mode)

Once you have your link:
1.  Open the link on your phone (Safari on iPhone, Chrome on Android).
2.  Tap the **Share** button (iOS) or **Menu** button (Android).
3.  Select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4.  It will now look and behave like a real app!

## Updating
If you make changes to the code locally:
- Just drag the folder to Netlify again to update it.
